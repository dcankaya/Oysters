How do we calculate the environmental impact of our products?

Using this cool thing called science! We have compiled data from peer-reviewed scientific articles from the leading universities, laboratories and research centres around the world which have calculated the environmental impacts of the production of different common items, such as 0.5 liter plastic bottles, .....

  • "The total greenhouse gas emission from textile production currently stands at 1.2 billion tonnes annually" according to Kiran Pandey
    • Greenhouse Gas Emissions
  • "17 to 20% of industrial water pollution is from textile dyeing and treatment"
    • Water Pollution
  • Gleick and Cooley found that producing bottled water requires between 5.6 and 10.2 million joules of energy per liter
    • Plastic Bottles
